关于我们 About

有很多次，在钢筋水泥构筑的高楼大厦之间，我感到无处可逃。后来才知道，真正的逃离并不发生在脚下，而是心里。或者，还有一种选择，不是去往城市的边缘，反而是中心的中心——在这里，胡同的咖啡店、茶馆、酒吧里，我找到了跟我一样的人，在生活反复的倾轧里，仍然愿意发现小小的美好，义无反顾地选择热爱生活。如果你愿意，在铺满银杏叶的天台上，我们终究可以和鸽子拥有同一片天空。

The real escape does not happen at the feet, but in the heart. Or, there is another option - not to go to the edge, but to the very center of the city. Here, in the coffee shops, tea houses and bars in the hutongs, I find people like me who constantly struggle in life still would like to discover the beauty of it and choose to love it without hesitation. Finally, we are able to share the sky with pigeons on the roof covered with ginkgo leaves.

My heart, the bird of the wilderness, has found its sky in your eyes. You smiled and talked to me of nothing, and I felt that for this I had been waiting long.
